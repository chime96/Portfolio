This map represents the oil and gas concession areas within the Niger Delta region of Nigeria. The digitalization was carried out to correct and refine the spatial extent of the concession boundaries. Upon reviewing the existing concession map, it was observed that several areas outside the core Niger Delta—such as Lagos and parts of eastern Nigeria—were mistakenly included. These areas are not traditionally or geographically part of the Niger Delta region, and their inclusion misrepresented the actual concession scope.The digitalization process was therefore necessary to ensure a more accurate geographic representation, focusing specifically on the true Niger Delta states (e.g., Rivers, Bayelsa, Delta, Akwa Ibom, Cross River, Edo, Abia, Imo, and Ondo). This refined version supports better regional planning, policy development, and resource management by aligning the map with the legitimate boundaries of the Niger Delta oil and gas production zones.
