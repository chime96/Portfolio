Al Kharsaah Solar Power Plant

The Al Kharsaah Solar Power Plant Digitization project involved the creation and management of a comprehensive geospatial database for one of Qatars largest solar energy installations. The main goal was to digitize, structure, and analyze spatial data for the plants infrastructure and environmental footprint.

ArcGIS Pro Digitization Solar Energy Cartography CAD to GIS

Niger Delta Oil&Gas Concession Map

This map represents the oil and gas concession areas within the Niger Delta region of Nigeria. The digitalization was carried out to correct and refine the spatial extent of the concession boundaries. Upon reviewing the existing concession map, it was observed that several areas outside the core Niger Delta—such as Lagos and parts of eastern Nigeria—were mistakenly included.

ArcGIS Pro Digitization Oil&Gas Blocks Cartography

Sultanate of Oman Mining Concession Map
